Keynote Speaker

MONICA ROTHGERY

Author and Keynote Speaker

From managing shifts at Taco Bell to making history as the first female Chief Operations Officer of KFC in the U.S., Monica has spent over 30 years in the restaurant industry, growing and inspiring those around her. She proudly became the first LGBTQ leader to ascend to the C-suite at Yum! Brands, one of the world's largest restaurant companies.

Monica's passion lies in empowering leaders to uncover their potential and make meaningful change-starting with themselves. A captivating speaker and coach, she weaves powerful stories into her presentations, entertaining and inspiring audiences worldwide.

Her debut book, Lessons from the Drive-Thru: Real Life Wisdom for Frontline Leaders, is an Amazon bestseller that shares insightful tales from her career to highlight the essence of true servant leadership.

When she's not working with organizations to empower their frontline, Monica enjoys life in Louisville, KY, with her wife Onyxe and their furry babies, Skye and Louis.

Workshop Leader

DAVID CHEN

Technology Innovator & Educator

With over 20 years of experience in Silicon Valley, David has led technology teams at some of the world's most innovative companies including Google and Tesla. His expertise in artificial intelligence and machine learning has revolutionized how companies approach digital transformation.

As a passionate educator, David has developed curriculum for leading universities and conducts workshops that bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in the tech industry.

His recent book, "The Innovation Mindset," explores how organizations can foster cultures of creativity and technological advancement in an increasingly competitive landscape.

David holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University and serves on the board of several tech startups. When not immersed in technology, he enjoys rock climbing and teaching coding to underprivileged youth in his community.

Panel Moderator

AMARA JOHNSON

Sustainability Expert & Consultant

Amara Johnson is a leading voice in sustainable business practices with a focus on helping organizations implement environmentally responsible operations without sacrificing profitability. She has advised Fortune 500 companies across diverse industries from manufacturing to hospitality.

As the founder of GreenStep Consulting, Amara has developed proprietary methodologies that have helped clients reduce their carbon footprint by an average of 35% while simultaneously improving operational efficiency.

Her dynamic speaking style and practical approach to sustainability have made her a sought-after keynote speaker and panel moderator at international conferences. Amara excels at making complex environmental concepts accessible and actionable for business leaders.

Amara holds an MBA from Harvard Business School and a Master's in Environmental Science from Yale. She serves on the UN Sustainable Development Advisory Board and has been recognized with numerous awards for her contributions to environmental leadership.
